!1 User Story

|As a hardware store owner
I want to view a list of my registered customers
So that I can contact them and make decisions based on customer volume.|

!1 Acceptance Criteria

|Visiting /customers should display a list of customers|
|The list should be sorted by email address|
|The list should display the total customer count at the bottom|
