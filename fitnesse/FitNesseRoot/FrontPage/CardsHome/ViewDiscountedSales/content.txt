!4 User Story

|!- As a hardware store owner
I want to be able view the total revenue reduction due to discounted inventory items during a specified month
So that I can better plan sales and promotions throughout the year
And not offer too many discounts in one month-!|

!4 Acceptance Criteria

|Visiting /discounttotals/MONTH should show the total discounted amounts, by order, for that month|
|MONTH should be represented as a number from 1 to 12|
|MONTH should support zero-padded and non-zero-padded forms|
|The list should show the order number, date and total discounted amount|
|The list should be sorted by date|
|The list should display the total discount amount at the bottom|

|A message should indicate if no discounted sales occurred that month|
